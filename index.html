About Stormwind

Stormwind City is the capital city of the Alliance. It is located in the northwestern part of Elwynn Forest, Azeroth. Formerly ruled by the young child king Anduin Wrynn (with the help of Highlord Bolvar Fordragon, the former regent, and Lady Katrana Prestor), it is now ruled over by King Varian Wrynn who returned during the time of the Lich King.


During the First War, the Kingdom of Azeroth, including its capital, Stormwind Keep, was utterly destroyed by the Horde and its survivors fled to Lordaeron. After the orcs were defeated at the Dark Portal at the end of the Second War, it was decided that the city would be rebuilt. The nobles of Stormwind assembled a team of the most skilled and ingenious stonemasons and architects they could find--which later turned sour and led to the rise of the Defias.


With the fall of the northern kingdoms, Stormwind is by far the most populated city in the world. It serves in many ways as the cultural and trade center of the Alliance, even with remote access to the sea. It is home to the Academy of Arcane Sciences, the only wizarding school in Eastern Kingdoms, as well as SI:7, a rogue intelligence organization.

 

Things to do

Always wanted to learn how to cook? Join Chef Robby Flay and whip up some delicious treats in the canals of Stormwind. You can learn everything from Spice Bread to Pickled Fangtooth and Nightfin Soup!

Grab the fishing rod and get some professional tips from the pros! Join Arnold Leland as he demonstrates techniques to catch alligators in the Stormwind canals!

Got a creative side? Head on over to the outer edge of the Trade District and see Theresa Denman. Learn how to make beautiful jewelery from Heavy Silver Rings to Crystal Citrine Necklaces!

Head on over to the Mage Quarter to see Georgio Bolero if you would like to learn some tailoring skills. Learn how to make cloaks, gloves, leggings, robes, slippers and much much more!

 

Sights to see

  • Stormwind Harbor: Transportation hub with boats to Darnassus and Borean Tundra.
  • The Mage Quarter: The Slaughtered Lamb (warlock trainers), herbalism, alchemy, tailoring, mage trainers, portals.
  • Trade District: Auction House, bank, flight trainer, guild vendors, hairdresser.
  • The Valley of Heroes: Entrance gates to Stormwind, with monuments to heroes from the Second War.
  • Old Town: Warrior, rogue, hunter trainers, center of SI:7, PvP vendors, Justice/Valor point vendors.
  • Stormwind Keep: Battlemasters, throne room with King Varian Wrynn and Genn Greymane, Library, garden.
  • The Dwarven District: Mining, blacksmithing, engineering trainers, forge/anvil, Auction House, Bank, entry to Deeprun Tram.
  • Cathedral Square: Home of the magnificent Cathedral of Light, City Hall, and the Orphanage. Paladin, priest, and first aid trainers. Gazebo and city cemetery added in Cataclysm.
  • The Eastern Earthenshrine: Portals to Cataclysm areas.
  • Stormwind Lake: replacement for the shattered Park district, destroyed by Deathwing

Where to stay

Trade District Inn

Located just inside the main gates of Stormwind City it is a busy hub with many things to do. Goods sold in the trade district include chesse, arcane goods and regeants, general goods, flowers, cloth/leather/mail armor, bows, guns and clothes. This area includes a bank, mailbox and an auction house.

Mage Quarter Inn

Nestled in the southwest corner of Stormwind, the Mage Quarter is home to the Slaughtered Lamb tavern as well as the Wizard's Sanctum. Throughout this quarter you can find herbalism, alchemy and tailoring related stores.

Old Town Inn

Stormwinds most ancient section. Many parts of it still predate the reconstruction of the city. It's a rustic, down-trodden place, but many Stormwind citizens feel it has its own unique charm. It is the residential area for most of the poor folk of the city. However, it is the location of the renowned Pig and Whistle Tavern.

Dwarven District Inn

The Dwarven District lies in the northern corner of Stormwind City. The forges in the district produce a constant haze, supplemented by the constant strokes of smiths' hammers. It is a center of commerce and industry, and the local factories work all night to supply Stormwind with all of it's goods.
